ASA24 Dataset Pipeline

1
FNDDS Database Prep
fndds_proc.py

Processes FNDDS Excel into a clean CSV for RAG embeddings. Expands abbreviations (NFS, NS) and formats food descriptions.

IN
2019-2020 FNDDS At A Glance - Foods and Beverages.xlsx
OUT
2019-2020 FNDDS - Foods and Beverages.csv
↓
2
NHANES Frequency Analysis
asa_proc.py

One-time analysis of NHANES dietary recall data across four survey periods (2011–2020). Identifies the top 1,000 most frequently consumed foods.

IN
NHANES/DR1IFF_G.XPT … DR2IFF_J.XPT (8 files)
OUT
NHANES/top_1000_frequent_select_foods.csv
↓
3
Portion Image Selection
asa_select_portions.py

For each of the top 1,000 foods, selects the largest, median, and smallest available ASA24 portion reference images (→ 3,000 rows total). Adds a PortionType label.

IN
top_1000_frequent_select_foods.csv data/ASA24_ImageMetadata.csv ASA24 portion images (local)
OUT
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_all_portions.csv
↓
4
Food Code Inference (RAG + Vision)
rag_food_code.py

GPT-4V describes each food image. MultiQueryRetriever (6 query variants, k=20) searches ChromaDB embeddings of FNDDS to find the best matching 8-digit food code. Includes checkpointing and exponential-backoff retry (5 iterations).

IN
FNDDS - Foods and Beverages.csv ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_all_portions.csv Portion image URLs
OUT
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_all_portions.csv
↓
5
Portion Size Estimation (Vision)
rag_portion_size.py

Two-stage vision estimation: (1) Shot mode — selects one of the available FNDDS portion options shown to the model; (2) Amount mode — asks model for a numeric quantity in the chosen unit. Parses fractions and normalises portion descriptions.

IN
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_all_portions.csv FoodWeights.csv Portion image URLs
OUT
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_portion.csv (+ GPTPortionDescription, GPTPortionAmount)
↓
5.5
Portion Weight Merge
merge_files.ipynb

Merges portion predictions with FoodWeights.csv to convert GPT portion descriptions into gram weights, producing the weight columns needed for nutrient calculation.

IN
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_portion.csv FoodWeights.csv
OUT
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_portion_with_weights.csv
↓
6
Nutrient Estimation
nutrient_estimate.py

Looks up nutrient values per 100 g from the FNDDS Excel sheet "FNDDS Nutrient Values". Applies formula: nutrient = (per_100g × weight_g) × 0.01. Produces both ground-truth-weight and GPT-weight nutrient columns.

IN
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_portion_with_weights.csv FNDDS Nutrient Values.xlsx
OUT
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_nutrition.csv (Energy kcal, Protein g, Carbs g, Fat g — label & GPT)
↓
7
Results Evaluation
eval_foodcode_portionsize.py

Evaluates food code accuracy (hierarchical matching: exact / close / far / mismatch by digit-prefix) and portion weight error (MAE, MAPE, median AE). Ground truth food codes and label weights come from the input CSV.

IN
ASA24_GPTFoodCodes_nutrition.csv FoodWeights.csv
OUT
Match class per row (exact/close/far/mismatch) MAE, MAPE, median AE (portion weight)

Nutrition5k Dataset Pipeline

1
FNDDS Database Prep
fndds_proc.py

Same as ASA24 pipeline — produces FNDDS CSV used for RAG embeddings. Run once; shared output.

IN
2019-2020 FNDDS At A Glance - Foods and Beverages.xlsx
OUT
2019-2020 FNDDS - Foods and Beverages.csv
↓
2
Dataset Preparation
nutrition5k_proc.py

Parses dish metadata from two café CSV files. Removes duplicate ingredient combinations, filters dishes whose rgb.png image is missing, then randomly selects 1,000 available dishes.

IN
dish_metadata_cafe1.csv dish_metadata_cafe2.csv Nutrition5k images ({dish_id}/rgb.png)
OUT
nutrition5k_dataset/metadata/dish_metadata_available.csv
↓
3
Per-Ingredient Food Code Inference
nutrition5k_food_code.py

Processes each dish image locally (rgb.png). GPT-4V identifies individual ingredients; for each ingredient, the RAG chain retrieves a matching 8-digit FNDDS code. Results stored in a semicolon-delimited per-ingredient format. Supports 5 retry iterations with checkpointing.

IN
dish_metadata_available.csv FNDDS - Foods and Beverages.csv {dish_id}/rgb.png (local images)
OUT
nutrition5k_results.csv (GPTFoodDescription — multi-line per ingredient) (GPTFoodCode — "ingr1: code1; ingr2: code2; …")
↓
4
Per-Ingredient Portion Estimation
nutrition5k_portion_size.py

For each ingredient in a dish, queries GPT-4V (looking at the full dish image) to estimate a numeric portion amount. Looks up the available FNDDS portion units from FoodWeights.csv for each food code. Stores results in a newline-delimited per-ingredient format. Parallel processing only.

IN
nutrition5k_results.csv FoodWeights.csv {dish_id}/rgb.png (local images)
OUT
nutrition5k_results.csv (updated) (+ GPTAmount — "ingr1: 1.5 cup\ningr2: 2 tbsp\n…")
↓
5
Nutrient Estimation (per-ingredient)
nutrient_estimate_mix.py

Parses per-ingredient portion strings from GPTAmount and GPTFoodCode. For each ingredient: looks up portion weight (g) from FoodWeights.csv, multiplies amount × unit weight, retrieves nutrient values per 100 g from FNDDS Excel, scales by actual weight. Sums nutrients across all ingredients in the dish.

IN
nutrition5k_results.csv FoodWeights.csv FNDDS Nutrient Values.xlsx
OUT
nutrition5k_final.csv (Energy kcal, Protein g, Carbs g, Fat g — summed across ingredients)
↓
6
Results Evaluation
eval_foodcode_portionsize.py

Same evaluation script as ASA24. Compares predicted food codes against Nutrition5k ground-truth ingredient codes (hierarchical matching). Computes portion weight errors (MAE, MAPE) against known dish weights.

IN
nutrition5k_final.csv FoodWeights.csv dish_metadata_available.csv (ground truth)
OUT
Match class per row (exact/close/far/mismatch) MAE, MAPE, median AE (portion weight)
